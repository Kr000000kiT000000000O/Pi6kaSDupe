About Michael Scofield

Michael Scofield is a fictional character from the television series Prison Break, portrayed by Wentworth Miller. He is a structural engineer with a genius-level intellect, who devises an elaborate plan to break his brother out of prison. His calm demeanor, coupled with his ability to anticipate every detail, makes him a strategic mastermind.

Key Skills

  • Strategic Planning: Always one step ahead, Michael's plans are incredibly detailed.
  • Problem Solving: Known for his quick thinking and innovative solutions.
  • Leadership: Despite being soft-spoken, he commands respect and trust from others.
  • Tattoo Mapping: Used a full-body tattoo as a blueprint for his escape plan.
